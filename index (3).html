ABOUT

For a small business, the company website can often be the difference between success and failure. Potential customers and clients visit webpages for company info, prices, products, and other information. A good website is user-friendly and provides easily-accessible information in an intelligent and sophisticated design. An outdated, slow, or unresponsive website is an immediate detractor to a small business, and can hurt the bottom line.

At Coded By Jake, I work directly with all of my clients in order to ensure that their needs are being met fully, and their expectations are being exceeded with every project that I work on. This includes face to face meetings, emails, phone calls, and design breifings are included every step of the way. My websites follow responsive design paradigms using a combination of different styling and grid techniques, which ensures that your business will always be well represented, whether it be on a phone, tablet, or computer.
